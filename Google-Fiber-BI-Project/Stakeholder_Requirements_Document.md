# Stakeholder Requirements Document: Google Fiber Repeat Call Analysis

**BI Professional:** Timilehin Afolayan
**Client/Sponsor:** Emma Santiago, Hiring Manager

## Business problem
The primary problem to be solved is understanding the frequency and drivers of repeat customer support calls across different markets and problem types. The Google Fiber Customer Service team needs to reduce call volume, increase customer satisfaction, and improve operational optimization. The central question to answer is: How often are customers repeatedly contacting the customer service team?

## Stakeholder usage details
The stakeholders will use the BI tool (dashboard) to evaluate how effectively the team resolves customer issues. They will explore how often customers are calling repeatedly across different markets (Market 1, Market 2, Market 3) and problem categories to identify problem areas and improve customer satisfaction.

## Primary requirements
The requirements that must be met by this BI tool for the project to be successful include:
*   A chart or table measuring repeat calls by first contact date.
*   A chart or table exploring repeat calls by market and problem type.
*   Charts showcasing repeat calls by week, month, and quarter.
*   Insights into the customer issues that generate more repeat calls.
*   Repeat caller trend analysis across the three market cities.
*   Charts that allow stakeholders to view trends by week, month, quarter, and year.
